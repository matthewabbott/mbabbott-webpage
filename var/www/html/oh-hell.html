Oh Hell Card Game

Learn More on Wikipedia View Source on GitHub

How to Play:

1. Each round, players are dealt an increasing number of cards (1 to 7)

2. Players bid on how many tricks they think they'll win

3. Get a point for each trick taken. Get 10 bonus points if you make your bid.

4. You must follow suit if possible

5. Highest card of the lead suit wins unless trumped

To play a card, drag it to the center area. On mobile, double tap instead.
